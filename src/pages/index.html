My name is Tatiana. I am a PT and Nutrition Consultant in Milton Keynes. After years in the marketing and sales industry, I made the decision to turn my passion for training into a full time career. I enjoy functional training and multi-joint exercises that require incorporating your entire core muscles. Having kickboxing and Thai boxing background I like to implement aspects of these sports into my training sessions. Throwing some punches and kicks is not only a great workout but it relieves stress. Participating in pole dance, tennis, football, swimming, skiing, snowboarding and horse riding throughout my life I have developed an understanding of a variety of sport-specific training principles. I am also an active MUM so if you struggle to get in shape after pregnancy I will help you to lose extra weight and become a fit and confident. With a safe and effective routine your body undergo amazing changes! I chose to live a healthy lifestyle because it makes me a better person. I wanted to be a true example to moms who went through the pregnancies that you can be healthy, beautiful, strong and driven! The most important whatever your goal is I will be there to motivate and support you so you will learn how to love yourself no matter what! If you want a personal trainer who is enthusiastic, motivational and highly qualified then look no further!
